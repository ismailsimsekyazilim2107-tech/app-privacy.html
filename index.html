Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Metal Detector Pro (Spectras Magnetic Sensor Tool) (“the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to providing a safe and transparent user experience.

1. Overview

This application does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information directly from users. The App is designed to function primarily offline, and all core processing is performed locally on the device.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect or request any of the following information:

  • Personal identifiers such as name, email address, or phone number
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Precise location data
  • Device storage or file system data

3. Device Sensor Usage

The App uses the device’s built-in magnetometer (magnetic field sensor) to measure and display magnetic field strength in real time.

All sensor data is processed locally on the device and is not stored, transmitted, or shared externally.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

This App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect limited non-personal data, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data and interactions
  • Approximate location (based on IP and user settings)

This data is used for:

  • Ad delivery and personalization
  • Performance measurement
  • Fraud prevention and security

Users can reset or disable their advertising ID in device settings.

5. In-App Purchases

The App may offer optional premium features through Google Play Billing.

All transactions are securely processed by Google Play. The developer does not access or store any payment information.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Play Billing (purchases)

Each service operates under its own privacy policy.

7. Children’s Privacy

This App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or store personal data, no user information is transmitted to external servers controlled by the developer.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page or within the Google Play listing.
