Privacy Policy

Metal Detector Pro (Spectras Magnetic Sensor Tool) respects user privacy.

This application does NOT collect, store, or directly transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

App Functionality

  • Uses the device’s built-in magnetic sensor (magnetometer)
  • Displays magnetic field strength and variations in real time
  • Works offline for core functionality

Advertisements

This app may display third-party advertisements (such as Google AdMob).

Ad providers may collect anonymous data such as device type, advertising ID, and basic usage data for ad personalization and analytics.

No personally identifiable information is collected by the app itself.

Premium Features

  • Optional premium features may be offered in the future
  • Premium features may remove ads or unlock additional functionality
  • No personal data is required to use premium features

Data Collection

The app does NOT collect personal data such as:

  • Name or email
  • Location information
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media
  • Microphone or camera access

All magnetic sensor data is processed locally on the device and is not stored or shared.

Third-Party Services

Advertising services used by this app may operate under their own privacy policies. Users can opt out of personalized ads through device settings.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. No personal data is knowingly collected from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if new features are added. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the email listed on Google Play.
